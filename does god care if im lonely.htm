Does God care if I’m lonely?

Even with alot of other people around, it is easy to feel completely alone sometimes. The world moves so quickly, and with all the busy-ness of life, do you ever wonder if you are important? Do you ask: Does anyone care about the details of my life? Would anyone even like me if they knew all the things I’ve done, or said, or even just thought? Does anyone  truly love me... for being me? The answer is YES! Definitely, absolutely, YES! Without a shadow of a doubt, there is someone who knows everything about you, and still loves you more deeply than you even love yourself. His name is Jesus, and He is more real than you may realize.  People say He was just a good person, a teacher, a carpenter. But He is much more than that. The Bible, which is the living Word of God, says that Jesus is the Son of God. Jesus sacrificed His own life so that we would not have to suffer the eternal consequences of our wrongdoing. Because of Jesus, we never have to be alone again. Because of Jesus, we have a future home with Him in heaven. Because of Jesus, we can experience  unconditional love.  You may wonder why He would love or even like you. After all, how can you love someone you don’t even know? Well, it’s because He does know you. He knows everything about you. The Bible says He even knows the number of hairs on your head, and that He knit you in your mother’s womb. He knows when you get up and go to sleep and everything in between. He knows you that well, and He couldn’t love you anymore than He already does. Even when you can’t love yourself because of things you have done or said or thought,  Jesus still does. He does not base how He feels towards you because of what you have done or will do. In fact, nothing you can do can change how He feels. He knows everything about you and welcomes you with open arms. This world is about reciprocation. We know the saying, “You scratch my back and I’ll scratch yours.” If you have something to offer that is worthwhile, then somehow that makes you more valuable. If you do something to upset or offend someone, they might not forgive you. People’s affections are fickle and often based on what  we do for them or how we make them feel.  But Jesus doesn’t think that way. He thinks you are great! In fact, He is happy just by the very thought of you. Nothing you do or don’t do could make Him love you any more or any  less. And that’s the truth.  Since He knows all about you and loves you more than anyone does or ever will: would you  like to get to know Him?
