Does God want me to be healed?

This is one of the most asked questions by new Christians and, sadly so, even those who have been Christians for a long time. Healing is a very important topic as it affects our present life on earth to a significant degree. When sick, we are unable to fulfill our duties like going to school or work, taking care of our families, etc. It robs us of our self-sufficiency  and makes us dependent on others around us. The Bible makes God’s will regarding healing very clear to us. Anyone who has taken time to read the Bible will be convinced of the fact that God does indeed want to heal ALL that are sick. We are told in Isaiah 53:5 that ‘we are healed by His stripes.’ If God does not desire to heal us, He would not have made provisions for our healing as outlined in the  scripture above.  In Matthew 8:2-3, we are presented with the story of the leper who comes to Jesus asking Jesus to heal him if Jesus was willing. Jesus’ response was, “I am willing... be ye healed.”  Jesus’ response leaves no doubt about His will for healing us. One of God’s redemptive Names in the Bible is “Jehovah Rapha” which means, “I am the Lord God that healeth thee.” It is absurd to believe that God will reveal His Name as the  “God that healeth thee” and not want to heal you.  Friends, it is God’s desire to heal you. CAN GOD HEAL ME? The Bible has many instances about Jesus going about and healing all that were sick. In Matthew 8:16 we are told that a leper approaches Christ and Jesus not only heals him of leprosy but also heals all others that were sick. The Bible didn’t mention all the types of illnesses that were healed because it would have been too many to elaborate in the Bible. As a matter of fact, it may not be as important as to what illness they had as it is that Jesus  healed ALL their diseases. We are told in Numbers 16:46-50 that “He brought them forth also with silver and gold, and there was not one feeble person among all their tribes.” These verses refer to the Israelites as they were delivered from the land of Egypt and it clearly states that not one of them was sick. God once again had healed all their diseases; there is not one of them that was sick.  Friends, it doesn’t matter what the illness is, Jesus Christ can heal ALL disease.
