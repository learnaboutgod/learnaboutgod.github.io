GOD Likes You!

Do you ever wonder what God thinks about you? If He likes you? Whether He cares about what you think, feel, or are going through right now? It is sad how so many live in anxiety, worried God will make them suffer because they believe He doesn’t like them. It is also sad how others think God is cold and indifferent, caring little if their life is filled with the deepest hurt or misery. Because the truth is, God cares... more than we know. You are absolutely priceless to Him, and the natural bent of His heart toward you is good, not harm. When God thinks of you, His heart is filled with the warmest affection, and the deepest love. God shares from His own heart that He handcrafted you with marvelous workmanship1-- only you have that special way of laughing, talking, and doing what you do. God is deeply interested in what you think and feel because you are His wonderful creation, with a unique personality all your own! And, though others may miss it, though people may tear you down because they only see a part of your heart, though some may ignore you because they have their own issues, God sees your true worth. You are beautiful in your own special way. God-- the most engaging, most beautiful, most perfect being of all-- loves you and delights in you more than you can possibly imagine! God wants you to know that “... He will take great delight in you; He will quiet you with His love; He will rejoice over you with singing.”2   God loves you so much that He actually likes you! And He wants to be in a rich, dynamic relationship with you so you can experience the fullness of His amazing love! How can you begin a beautiful relationship with this God?
