Does God care if I’m lonely?

Even with a lot of other people around, it is easy to feel completely alone sometimes. The world moves so quickly, and with all the busy-ness of life, do you ever wonder if you are important? Do you ask: Does anyone care about the details of my life? would anyone even like me if they knew all the things I’ve done, or said, or even just thought? Does anyone truly love me... for being me?
Part of getting to know someone is finding out what they think, feel, and like. As you get to know them better, you see what they are about and what stirs their heart! It’s no different when it comes to God. Though God is so awesome and infinite that we cannot begin to truly understand Him with our limited faculties, He shares: “Let not the wise man boast of His wisdom, or the strong man boast of his strength, or the rich man boast of his riches, but let him who boasts, boast about this, that he understands and knows Me, that I am the Lord who exercises kindness, justice, and righteousness on the earth, for in these I delight, declares the Lord.” (Jeremiah 9:23 & 24, The Bible, NIV) This is incredible! The God of all creation wants us to know Him! Like any good relationship, He wants us to know Him more and more, even as He knows us... it’s just, there’s alot of Him to know!

Does God care about me?

Does it feel like you have to impress God for you to matter to Him? If so, maybe you are thinking like an adult, rather than a child. In Matthew 18:3, Jesus says that unless we become like a little child, we won’t be able to enter the Kingdom of Heaven.

Does God want to forgive me?

Knowing God is knowing forgiveness. They are one and the same. The more we understand the nature of forgiveness,

the more we understand God. If someone asks, “What is God like?” look at Jesus. He prayed for those who tried to

destroy Him. Forgiveness and love touch, taste, feel, and smell the same. They have the same effects.

Does God want me to be healed?

This is one of the most asked questions by new Christians and, sadly so, even those who have been Christians for a long time. Healing is a very important topic as it affects our present life on earth to a significant degree. When sick, we are unable to fulfill our duties like going to school or work, taking care of our families, etc. It robs us of our self-sufficiency and makes us dependent on others around us.  If God is good, why do bad things happen to me? Why do bad things happen to good people? We all face times that threaten to crush the life out of us, don’t we? Maybe you’re in one of those times right now, where you’ve lost someone dear, or had something precious unjustly taken from you. Maybe you’ve just received devastating news from the doctor, or had your world smashed to pieces by betrayal or rape. In your pain, in your frustration, in your anger, you may have asked, “God, if You are good, how could You allow this? Why didn’t You stop it? Do You really care  about me? Really, do You?”
