God is All-Powerful

Huge fantastic ideas may stir in my mind, but that doesn’t mean I have the power to make them happen. But what

God wants to create, He has the ability to create. God is all powerful! The prophet Jeremiah declares: “Ah, Sovereign

LORD, You have made the heavens and the earth by Your great power and outstretched arm. Nothing is too hard for

you.” (Jeremiah 32:17, The Bible, NIV 1984)

Science has shown that our world, the stars, the galaxies, and the whole multiverse had a beginning point (termed

the “Big Bang” by scientists), verifying the truth of what God shared with us in Genesis 1:3 of the Bible. Each of these

celestial entities is made of a massive amount of energy. Since energy cannot be created or destroyed (it can only

change forms), where did all this energy come from in the beginning? Before the multiverse was; even before any

atoms, subatomic particles, or the forces that govern their interaction existed; God was. God is eternal, without a

beginning or an end, and He is so powerful in and of

Himself that even His voice (His breath and His word) is

imbued with energy. All God had to do was speak, and,

as His words went forth, energy was released to bring

all these things into existence. It’s one thing to dream a

gigantic dream; it’s another thing to be able to make it

a reality. But God is that powerful: so powerful, in fact,

that He is omnipotent (someone who within Himself has

limitless power). No other being, entity, or force-- no

demon, no star, no human, not satan, nothing else-- comes close to even an infinitesimally small bit of God’s power!

The Bible not only revealed long ago that our cosmos had a starting point, but that “... by Him (Jesus) all things were

created: things in heaven and on earth, visible and invisible... all things were created by Him and for Him. He is

before all things, and in Him all things hold together.” (Colossians 1:16b,d,17, NIV) The Apostle John tells us: “And I

heard, as it were, the voice of a great multitude, as the sound of many waters and as the sound of mighty

thunderings, saying, ‘Alleluia! For the Lord God Omnipotent reigns!’” (Revelation 19:6, The Bible, NKJV)

God’s power is seen in all that surrounds us, but also in His ability to change your life. Whether you’re trapped in an

addiction like alcohol, drugs, or pornography; struggling with depression, anorexia, or suicide because things seem so

tough and hopeless; or successful on the outside with the money, car, popularity, spouse, and kids, but laden with

emptiness or insignificance on the inside; the good news is God is powerful enough to bring freedom, significance, joy,

and peace into your life! The key is realizing deep down that you need help so bad that you invite Jesus into your

heart as your Savior and the One who gets to lead your life. (If you’re worried about giving up control, don’t. He’s

God. He loves you. And, who wouldn’t want to follow the One who knows the best path to lasting joy and

satisfaction?) At that point, He puts His very Spirit, the Holy Spirit, in you to lead you and fill you with purpose, love,

and the power to overcome! Jesus loves to make all things new, and He has the power to do it, if you let Him!

Who is God?
