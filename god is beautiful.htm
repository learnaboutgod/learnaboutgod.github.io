God is Beautiful

Has your heart ever fluttered at the sight of a beautiful woman or a handsome guy? The prettiest gal you could not

take your eyes off of, the cutest guy you were drawn to, the most moving sunset you have beheld, are really just

shadows of the stunning radiance and beauty of God. The psalmist in his wisdom and understanding expresses as

his greatest desire: “One thing have I asked of the Lord, that will I seek... and [insistently] require: that I may

dwell in the house of the Lord [in His presence] all the days of my life, to behold and gaze upon the beauty [the

sweet attractiveness and the delightful loveliness] of the Lord...” (Psalm 27:4a,c, the Bible, Amplified)

God is a being of infinite beauty. Everything about God is beautiful. His thoughts. His actions. All He has created.

His very being. He is the essence of beauty itself. And beauty finds its very source in God. Men and women through

the ages have been inspired to legendary feats of daring do for gorgeous people and pleasing things, because we

were made in our heart of hearts to be stirred by beauty. We are enamored with it. Yet, the longing in your soul

for beauty is really at its core a longing for God, and can only find its ultimate fulfillment in relationship with

the most beautiful of beautifuls. God’s beauty is not skin deep (Isaiah 53:2, The Bible, NIV), it is His heart-- the

purity of His love-- and nothing can fully fulfill our desire for beauty except the beauty of the divine. It is why

no matter how many beautiful earthly things some may possess, it is never enough to satisfy and satiate the desire

for even more. God is unequaled and unrivaled in all He is. His loveliness simply dwarfs the glory of all others, and all

others combined.

To be in the presence of such unfettered beauty would leave us breathless. To be liked by one of such beauty would

ascribe to us incredible value and worth.

Yet, the crazy truth is that God likes you, loves you, and wants to be in relationship with you! You were made to be in

relationship with beauty... the most beautiful... you were made to be in relationship with God Himself!

Who is God?
