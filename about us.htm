What We Believe

We believe in ONE GOD; knowing infinite and perfect joy and love and fellowship within Himself, God created us to know Him and His love for us so that we would be filled with the joy of loving Him and experiencing His salvation in our lives.  God is one in three distinct persons –eternally existing as Father, Son and Holy Spirit, yet who is one in being, essence and glory:  God the FATHER – He is Creator of all, the master architect of the universe, this world, and us.  He is sovereign, all powerful, deserving of all glory and honor.  His nature perfectly good, holy, just and righteous.  He demonstrated His love for us and expression of grace by sending His only Son, Jesus Christ, whom He loved, into the world at infinite cost to Himself to redeem sinful and lost man.  He enabled us to live spiritual lives on earth by sending the Helper, or Comforter, the Holy Spirit.  God the SON –  JESUS CHRIST, who came in the flesh, and was conceived by Holy Spirit,  is God who came to us in the flesh, through a Virgin birth, and lived a perfectly sinless life.  He is God in human form, made in the image of His Father, and who, without ceasing to be God, became man in order to demonstrate who God is and provide the means of salvation for all humanity.  He did this through His death, resurrection and ascension to the right hand of God the Father.  He redeemed us from lives of death and separation from God due to our sins by dying on the cross in our place, paying the penalty we deserved for our disobedience.  He is full of grace and truth; He reveals the Father and honors the Father in all things; He jealously defends the Spirit; He will return in power and glory to judge all sin and evil, to restore creation to a place without disease, pain, suffering or death, and to rule over all as King.  God the HOLY SPIRIT – He is the Third Person of the Trinity; He is fully God, and regenerates sinners, and lives in believers.  He is the seal by whom the Father guarantees our salvation.  He is called the Helper, the Comforter, the Advocate, the Counselor, the Spirit of God, the Spirit of Christ, and the Spirit of truth.   He convicts us of sin, righteousness and judgment; He reveals to us our need for a Savior.  He baptizes and indwells us at salvation to set us apart and make us new; He empowers us to live the Christian life and be transformed by applying the gospel of grace to every area of our lives:  o He anoints us to enable us to witness and grow in loving and serving Jesus; o He fills us and empowers us to share the gospel and disciple others; o He reveals Jesus and brings glory to Jesus; o He leads us in worshipping God in Spirit and in truth; o He is the giver of spiritual gifts so that the Church, the Body of Christ, by the unity of the Spirit may be built up today. Every believer is called to live so in the power of the indwelling Spirit that he will not fulfill the lust of the flesh but will bear fruit to the glory of God. SALVATION AND THE GOSPEL OF TRUTH We believe in ONE WAY to receive SALVATION: forgiveness through the blood of Christ offered as a propitiation for the sins of mankind, received exclusively by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.  It is a gift of God’s grace through faith in the finished work of Jesus Christ’s death on the cross to redeem us from death caused by our sins.  Salvation is received by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.  Just as salvation cannot be earned by good works, neither does it need good works to be maintained or sustained.  Once saved, we are a new creation, and good works are a natural result of our changed life.  It is the privilege of all who are born again of the Spirit to be assured of their salvation from the very moment in which they trust Christ as their Savior. This assurance is not based on human merit or achievement, but is produced by the witness of the Holy Spirit, who confirms God’s truth in the life of the believer. We believe in the GOSPEL for it is the power of God to save all who believe. The gospel’s truth says that because of God’s love for us, at infinite cost to Himself, Jesus gave Himself to the curse of sin, suffering the shame and death that each of us should have instead, so that we wouldn’t have to, and lived the sinless life that none of us could have, to earn God’s blessing of eternal life for us. The gospel is the power of God to save all who believe in the sense that by His blood, a life of repentance for sin and faith in Jesus as Savior and Lord alone, not by works whatsoever but only by His grace, we are saved and made spiritually a new creation by Holy Spirit who takes up indwelling residence in our hearts eternally. Faith in Jesus is eternal life by grace and salvation from sin’s curse of eternal death. GOD’S WORD We believe in the HOLY BIBLE, that they are the very words of God revealed to man through inspiration of Holy Spirit – it is His revelation to those who place their hope and trust in Him.  God’s Word is without error, carrying supreme authority in matters of Christian faith and conduct; and necessary for knowing and obeying God – useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness. While still using the individual writing styles of the human authors, the Holy Spirit perfectly guided them to ensure they wrote precisely what He wanted written, without error or omission. GOD’S COMMISSION The Lord Jesus Christ commands all believers to proclaim the Gospel throughout the world and to disciple men of every nation. The fulfilment of that Great Commission requires that all worldly and personal ambitions be subordinated to a total commitment to "Him who loved us and gave Himself for us."  It is the obligation of all believers to witness, by word and life, to the truth and reality of God’s Word. We are to preach God’s gospel of grace to all the world.
