We all have a story to tell-- of the exhilaration we felt the first time we fell in love, of the crazy things we did to try

to see the giant sea turtles, of the struggles we endured and how they indelibly

changed us.

Stories are the snapshots of our life that tell of who we were, who we are, and

who we’re

becoming. We connect with one another through our stories of happiness and

failure, discovery and defeat, because we are relational beings. Stories touch us

at our very core and help us to understand, know, and love.

Some of the most meaningful and significant stories ever told were from Jesus, who shared His

experience of God the Father and God the Spirit from even before time began. He shared so

much from His heart about love, heaven, us, and other spiritual realities that is so life-giving in

helping us understand God, ourselves, and our world. We’d encourage you to read Matthew’s

journal of his experience of Jesus and the stories and truths he heard Jesus share.

What follows are the stories of some people we trust and love as to how they met God,

and the amazing love, light, and freedom He’s brought into their lives. We hope you

find all of them helpful in your own journey to know and connect with God.
